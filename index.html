The name "Mittukhedi" reflects the village's deep connection to the soil. Founded over a century ago by hardworking Dangi farming families, the village has evolved from a small settlement into a close-knit community in Rajgarh district. The village embodies the spirit of "Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam" (The world is one family).

Mittukhedi is centered around a main road, with smaller lanes branching out. The heart of the village, the Chowk, features an old Neem tree and a platform for community gatherings.
